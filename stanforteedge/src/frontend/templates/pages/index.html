Our portfolio Africa

project Enable Africa

Project Enable Africa is a community development initiative that was founded in 2014 to advocate for the rights and empowerment of persons with disabilities in Nigeria. We believe that everyone deserves good quality life and that no one should be discriminated against on the account of their disability.

StudentPad

StudentPad envisions all Nigerian student employability. Our mission is to prepare, equip and facilitate the transition of undergraduates from school to existing and emerging industries.

Lafiami

Lafiami is a user-friendly digital platform that aggregates regular and special health insurance products in Nigeria so that you can easily find, compare and buy suitable, affordable and accessible insurance products based on your personal data including income, location, age and health history. We make access to health insurance easier for you so that you can enjoy quality and reliable healthcare all year round.

check naija

Check Naija is a broad spectrum fact-checking website with verifying social media hoaxes and scams circulating in Nigeria.

FollowDisACT

The FollowDisACT Campaign coined from the words "Follow Disabilities Act" is a 5 year campaign project to coordinate a national conversation on the Nigerian Disabilities Act of 2019.
